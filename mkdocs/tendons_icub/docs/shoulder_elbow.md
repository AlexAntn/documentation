## 2. Left shoulder

<img src='../img/shoulder/ICUB_SHOULDER_LEFT.png' height=500px width = auto>


!!! note "Rules for a good start"
    1.Always remove the broken cable

    2.Pay attention of putting the tensionner upside down so that the crimp dont exit

    3.Keep in Mind that the shoulder is a set of 4 pairs of antagonistic cables : (1-2), (3-4), (5-6), (7,8) and to be able to pt bakc in place one pair of cable you need to make sure that the tensionner for both cables are looseMake sure that you have their tensionner with the more space possible


### 2.1 Left Shoulder Rotation Cable 1

![ICUB_SHOULDER_LEFT](img/shoulder/ICUB_SHOULDER_LEFT_Cable_1.png)

> First remove the tensionner from the bloc pulleys in order to fit the crimp of one side of the cable.

> Replace the tensionner with the cable inside it

!!! tip
    Always face the cavity of the crimp on a closed side in order to do NOT let the cable exit.
